Out with the Old, In with the New.

The first thing you'll notice about the app is how completely different it looks. GBA4iOS 2.0 was designed from the ground up for iOS 7, with the goal of making everything not only look better, but simpler to use. Taking this a step further, GBA4iOS now has a built-in web browser for downloading games, making it much easier to start playing your favorite Game Boy Advance games. Plus, 2.0 has been fully optimized for iPads, giving Apple's tablet a first class emulation experience.

With GBA4iOS, cheaters always win.

The number one most requested feature is finally here: GBA4iOS 2.0 has full support for cheats! Playing some classic games are nice, but cheats give you a whole new level of freedom with what you can do. With support for five different cheat code formats, you don't have to worry about whether your code is compatible with GBA4iOS - it just works. Better still, per-game Master Codes are handled for you automatically by GBA4iOS, making the cheating experience just that much easier.

Turn iPhone Gaming, into Console Gaming.

Touch screens are great. They adapt to whatever they need to be, allowing GBA4iOS to display a customized interface for each game you want to play. However, for some games nothing beats having physical controls. Luckily, iOS 7 brings support for external controllers to be used for games, and GBA4iOS takes full advantage of this. Just connect your controller - either physically or wirelessly - to your iOS device, and GBA4iOS will automatically start responding to the buttons you press. Combine this with the ability to output the game's screen over an HDMI cable or AirPlay, and you have all the components needed for a truly console-like experience, right in your pocket.
